About

Author in Shalakya Tantra and Ayurvedic Ophthalmology.

Dr. Narayan Vidwansa is a Professor as well as Author in Shalakya Tantra and Ayurvedic Ophthalmology, graduated in BAMS from Nagpur University, Nagpur (MH.) & He did Ph.D from Pune University.

He had 37 Years of teaching experience including 17 years to Post Graduate student. Former HOD, Shalakya Tantra at Vidarbha Ayurved Mahavidyalaya, Amravati (MH), Former Professor at Sumatibhai Shah Ayurved College.

Ex Member, Undergraduate and post graduate Clinical Board of studies, Maharashtra University of Health Sciences Nashik(MH), Also worked as a resource person in many Teacher's Reorientation programmes.
